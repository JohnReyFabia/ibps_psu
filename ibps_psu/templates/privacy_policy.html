Privacy Policy



Information We Collect

To facilitate your participation in our study, we gather two categories of data. Firstly, we collect user account information during the registration process. This information includes your full name, email address, and an encrypted password for secure access. Additionally, you have the option to provide your mailing address and phone number, although these details are not mandatory. Secondly, as part of the study, you will be asked to respond to a carefully crafted set of questions designed to identify burnout symptoms. It's important to note that these survey responses are held in strict confidence and are only accessible to authorized administrators within the Palawan State University Office of Student Affairs and Services (PSU-OSAS). We take your privacy seriously and have implemented stringent security measures to safeguard your information. Your participation in this study ensures that your responses and personal details remain confidential while contributing valuable insights to our research efforts.



How We Use Your Information

We place a high priority on the secure and ethical utilization of the data we collect in the Identification of Burnout Presence to the Students of Palawan State University with Automated Responses as Reinforcement through Natural Language Processing project. Here's an in-depth look at how your information is utilized:

  • User Authentication: Your account information is used for user authentication and to ensure that only registered students of Palawan State University can participate in the study.
  • Survey Responses: Your survey responses are collected to assess and identify the presence of burnout. These responses are confidential and are used solely for research and support purposes.

By participating in our study and providing your survey responses, you are contributing directly to the advancement of knowledge surrounding student well-being. Your trust in our research process allows us to work diligently towards improving the overall academic experience at Palawan State University. We want to assure you that your data remains confidential, and we are deeply appreciative of your invaluable participation in this significant research effort.

Cookies

As part of our commitment to providing you with an optimized and personalized experience on our platform, we employ the use of cookies. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you interact with our website. These files contain information that helps us recognize you during your subsequent visits, enhancing the overall user experience. It's important to note that cookies do not store personal information; rather, they enable us to tailor your interaction with our platform to your preferences.

Cookies serve several essential functions, such as remembering your login credentials for a streamlined login process, retaining your settings and preferences, and enabling us to track and analyze website traffic. They also assist in providing you with relevant content and recommendations based on your past interactions. However, we respect your privacy and provide you with the option to manage your cookie settings. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings. However, it's important to be aware that disabling cookies may impact the full functionality of our platform, potentially limiting certain features and personalization options. We recommend considering the balance between privacy preferences and the benefits of a tailored and efficient user experience when making your choice regarding cookie management.



Third party links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites or services. These third-party websites have their own distinct privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their data practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party websites before using them. However, it's important to note that within our platform, we establish a connection to the corporate email accounts of Palawan State University students solely for the purpose of facilitating secure communication and record-keeping within the Palawan State University Office of Student Affairs and Services (PSU-OSAS). This connection is established to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of our research and support initiatives while ensuring that all communications and records are maintained within the confines of the PSU-OSAS administration. Rest assured that this connection is implemented with the utmost care and adherence to privacy and security standards, and it is exclusively utilized for the designated administrative purposes.
